Jessica Laze Bio

Jessica Laze is a senior studying Supply Chain Management at Michigan State University. Throughout her years studying at Michigan State, she developed a passion for the automotive industry and made it her goal to work full time in it after college. In the summer of 2021, Jessica completed an internship at General Motors and worked for their Logistics Team mainly managing Oceanic Freight. She will be returning to General Motors after she graduates in 2022 and will participate in their Global Purchasing and Supply Chain TRACK Program. Jessica has also developed an interest in information technology throughout her years at university and plans to complete the minor.

The classes Jessica is taking this semester include:

  • SCM 373
  • MKT 310
  • ITM 311
  • SCM 460
  • MI 349
